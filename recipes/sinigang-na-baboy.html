Sinigang na Baboy

Sinigang na Baboy - Filipino soup, made mainly with pork belly and tamarind!

Other ingredients include but not limited to: Tomato, String Beans, Water Spinach, Radish, Taro, and Onion.

Ingredients:

  • 1 kilo pork belly (sliced into serving pieces)
  • 1 large Onion (sliced)
  • 4 large tomatoes (cubed)
  • Plain water (as needed)
  • Water Spinach (leaves only)
  • String beans (slice 2 inches)
  • Radish (cut diagonally)
  • Tomato (quarter)
  • Taro (cubed)
  • Tamarind or Sinigang sa Sampaloc Mix
